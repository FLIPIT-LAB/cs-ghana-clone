ABOUT US

Cross Switch Ghana

Cross Switch Ghana LTD, formerly known as Alias Solutions Limited, is a payment service provider offering merchants and businesses multiple channels to accept and process payments from customers. In addition to our core billing and payments products, we have developed custom core and middleware infrastructure that enable financial service providers to provide digital financial products and services to previously excluded groups.

Cross Switch Ghana LTD is an entiity of Cross Switch International S.A.R.L, a global financial network, with a robust payment platform powering international inbound and outbound payments for its entities across Africa.
